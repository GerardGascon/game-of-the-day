Game of the Day

Game of the Day is a small initiative to showcase a small free game each day.

The game that gets showcased is selected entirely random, from a pool of games I like. Even I don't know which game is getting showcased tomorrow!

What you may find here

  • I try to limit the number of games to 3 games per developer. I want to showcase a variety of creators, not a variety of games from one person.
  • All games are free (or a pay what you want). I want these games to be played by anyone.
  • No demos, all games are full games.
  • No ads or any kind of in-app purchases. I find them uncomfortable and disturbing.
  • Any kind of AI-generated content. No need to explain why.
  • All games showcased are small games that normally do not surpass the 30-minute duration.

Do you have a game you like?

Cool! If you find a game you like and would like it to be shown on the website, please email me at hello@gerardgascon.com. If I like that, maybe eventually it will be showcased here.
