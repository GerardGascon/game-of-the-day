Game of the Day

Game of the Day is a small initiative to promote a small free game each day.

The game that gets showcased is selected entirely random. Even I don't know which game is getting showcased tomorrow!

Submit Your Game

If you would like your game to be showcased at some point, you can submit it yourself, and it will be included as soon as possible!

The game can be in any language you want. English is preferred so everyone can understand, but you are completely free to express yourself with the language you prefer.

Submit a form through this link with the following contents:

  • Your game's title.
  • A cover image. (16:9, any other aspect ratio will be cropped)
  • A short description of your game. (120 characters max)
  • A maximum of 3 screenshots from your game.
  • A link to your game's page.
  • Name and link to socials from all the developers of the game.

Restrictions

  • Your game can be web-based or at least have a downloadable Windows build.
  • You can submit up to 3 games. If you want to add another game, you will need to specify which one to remove.
  • The game must be free. (It can be a pay what you want starting at $0)
  • No demos, it must be a full game.
  • No ads or any kind of in-app purchases.
  • Any kind of AI-generated content is strictly forbidden.
  • You must have the legal right to use all the assets present within your game.
  • Your game should not surpass the 30-minute duration.

Disclaimer

All the submissions are entirely reviewed by me, and I reserve the right to reject the games if they don't completely fit the website's theme.
